Maharana Pratap

Maharana Pratap Jayanti is an important day for the people of Rajasthan as it marks the birth anniversary of Maharana Pratap, one of the greatest warriors and rulers of the state. Maharana Pratap was born on May 9, 1540, in Kumbhalgarh, Rajasthan, and his bravery and valour are still celebrated today.
